Retirement Accounts

Remember - not your keys, not your bitcoin! Self directed IRAs allow you to hold your private keys. All that is necessary is creating a corporate entity and registering as a qualified custodian. If you are unsure of how to accomplish this, contact a local lawyer and they should be able to help you with the paperwork for a minor fee
