Bitcoin Classes

If you're looking for structured sets of lecture materials and challenges, one of the many online courses may be helpful. Most of the online courses are free or fairly low cost.


Non-technical Online Classes

  • Bitcoin for Everybody (Saylor Academy, Course)
  • Blockchain & Money (MIT, Course)
  • Cryptocurrency Engineering & Design (MIT, Course)
  • Game Theory 101 Full Course (Video Course, advanced)
  • Learn Bitcoin Playfully (Online)
  • Learn Me A Bitcoin Lessons (Video Tutorials)
  • Plan B Network (Courses, Free and Paid)

Technical Online Classes

  • Bitcoin 101 Blackboard Series (7+ hours of Video Tutorials)
  • Bitcoin & Cryptocurrency Technologies (Princeton, Video Tutorials)
  • Bitcoin Developer Tutorials (Video Tutorials)
  • Elliptic Curve Cryptography for Bitcoin (13-episode video series)
  • Bitcoin for Developers I (Saylor Academy, Course)
  • Bitcoin Script Lessons (Bitscript, Lessons)
  • Bitcoin Protocol Development Curriculum (Github Resources, Chaincode Labs)
  • Rust Fundamentals for Bitcoiners (BTCdemy, Online Course)
  • Chaincode Online Seminars (Chaincode Labs, Online Course BTC/Lightning)
  • Coursera Cryptocurrency Course (Princeton, Online Course and over 61 videos)
  • Decoding Bitcoin (Chaincode Labs, Online Course)
  • Introduction to Digital Currency (University of Nicosia, Online MOOC)
  • Khan Academy Bitcoin Course (Khan Academy, Video Course)
  • MITOpenCourseware(MIT, Multiple Free Bitcoin Courses and Problem Sets)
  • PlebDevs(PlebDevs, Online Courses)
  • SuperTestnet Workshops (Bitcoin Script, BISQ, Nostr, Coinjoin Videos)
  • Bitcoin or How I Learned to Stop Worrying and Love Crypto (Udemy, 8 hour Video Tutorial)
